## Claim 2: Quantum superposition defeats adaptive adversaries
Evidence:
- Selector uses a quantum circuit with H and CX gates, measured at runtime, logged in [layer2_log.csv](layer2_log.csv).
- Compiled circuit operation evidence (`h`, `cx`, `measure`) and depth values in [layer2_log.csv](layer2_log.csv).
- Eavesdrop scenario escalation to CRITICAL in [layer6_log.csv](layer6_log.csv), with abort action in integration output logs [qass_integration_log.csv](qass_integration_log.csv).
- Threat and assumption model formalized in [security_model.md](security_model.md).

## Claim 3: Quantum ratchet provides quantum perfect forward secrecy
Evidence:
- Five consecutive ratchet advances logged with distinct ratchet info labels in [layer4_log.csv](layer4_log.csv): `ratchet_0` to `ratchet_4`.
- Ratchet divergence visualization in [qass_ratchet_key_divergence.png](qass_ratchet_key_divergence.png).
- Session key entropy stability in [qass_session_key_entropy.png](qass_session_key_entropy.png).
- Demo run evidence in [qass_integration_log.csv](qass_integration_log.csv) and console output confirming all five keys are distinct.
- Ratchet independence and Hamming-distance confidence intervals in [statistical_validation_report.md](statistical_validation_report.md).
- Ablation comparison with Layer 4 removed in [ablation_report.md](ablation_report.md) and [qass_ablation_plot.png](qass_ablation_plot.png).

- PQC swap (Kyber1024 -> Kyber768) with successful decryptions in [qass_integration_log.csv](qass_integration_log.csv) for `demo5_kyber1024` and `demo5_kyber768`.
- Cipher performance comparison in [qass_cipher_comparison.png](qass_cipher_comparison.png).
- Repeated-trial encryption reliability with confidence intervals in [statistical_validation_report.md](statistical_validation_report.md).
- Cross-mode correctness stability in [ablation_report.md](ablation_report.md).
